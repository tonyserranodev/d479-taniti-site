Accommodations in Taniti

Hotels

From family-owned inns to a luxurious four-star resort, Taniti has a wide range of accommodations to suit every traveler. Whether you prefer budget-friendly hostels or charming bed and breakfasts, all lodging is regularly inspected for comfort and quality.

Cuisine

Taniti offers ten restaurants with a variety of dining experiences: fresh local seafood and rice, classic American meals, and delicious Pan-Asian flavors. There‚Äôs something for every palate on the island.

Transportation

Public buses run through Taniti City from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m., while taxis and rental cars are available near the airport. Bikes are also available and the island is walkable, especially around Merriton Landing.
