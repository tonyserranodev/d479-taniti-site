Snorkeling

The clear, warm waters around Taniti are home to vibrant coral reefs and diverse marine life, making it a prime spot for snorkeling. Tours often depart from Merriton Landing and provide gear, guidance, and a chance to see colorful fish, sea turtles, and even rays in their natural habitat.

Nightlife

Taniti comes alive at night with a variety of entertainment options. Visitors can enjoy live music at local pubs, sample craft brews at a microbrewery, dance at a popular new club, or watch a movie. Many nightlife venues are located in Merriton Landing, a bustling area near Yellow Leaf Bay.
