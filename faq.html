FAQ

Common questions about visiting Taniti

What type of electrical outlets are used in Taniti?

Power outlets in Taniti are 120 volts, the same as in the United States.

What are the alcohol service hours and age restrictions?

Alcohol service is prohibited between midnight and 9:00 a.m. The legal drinking age is 18, though enforcement is relatively relaxed.

Will I be able to communicate in English?

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English, particularly in urban areas. However, English is less common in rural areas, especially among older residents.

What medical facilities are available?

Taniti has one main hospital with multilingual staff and several clinics throughout the island.

How safe is Taniti for tourists?

Violent crime is very rare on Taniti. However, with increasing tourism, visitors should be aware of occasional pickpocketing and petty crimes.

What should I know about holiday closures?

Taniti observes numerous national holidays. Many tourist attractions and restaurants close during these times, so it's important to plan your visit accordingly.

What currency is accepted in Taniti?

The U.S. dollar is the primary currency. Many businesses also accept euros and yen. Major credit cards are widely accepted, and currency exchange is available at several banks.
